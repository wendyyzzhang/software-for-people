Purple Cake

  • April 8th, 2023. for my 20th birthday

    • This is a taro-okinawan sweet potato infused into whipped cream, chiffon cake base. There’s no food coloring in this recipe— the vibrant color comes from the Okinawa sweet potato infusion. The recipe is scribbled down on a stained page in the back of our Vitamix manual, but she rarely needs it. We’ve made this cake at least one hundred times, and it is always the same: she bakes the cake, and I decorate it. It is a cake for every occasion— birthdays and baby showers and unexpected cake cravings and housewarmings and showing up to your neighbor’s house uninvited.
